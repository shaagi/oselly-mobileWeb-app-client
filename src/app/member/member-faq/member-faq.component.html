Frequently Asked Questions

What is Oselly?

Oselly makes it easier for you to play indoor basketball with your friends by letting you book gym time, invite friends, and split the payment all in one place.

Why do I have to give my debit/credit card to be in the game?

So the rental can be split equally if this game gets enough people. This prevents the host from having to chase people for money, and prevents non-hosts from overpaying. If not enough people are in nobody gets charged a dime.

What if I later learn that I can't make it?

You can hit the "sell my spot" button in your fulfilled games. This will generate a link that you or anyone else in the game can send to friends to replace you. If someone buys your spot 2+ hours before the game starts you don't get charged.

When do I pay?

If the game gets enough people, 2 hours before the game starts. If the game doesn't get enough people, you won't be charged

Can I invite my friends?

After you get in the game, yes. Before that, no.
